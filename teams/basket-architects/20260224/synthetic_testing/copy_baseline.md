## Problem

Do you ever forget what you've done? It can be hard to remember all the restaurants, bars, trips, and activities you've experienced. Most people don't have a good way to keep track.

---

## Solution

Experience Architects lets you log and rate your experiences. You can rate restaurants, bars, activities, trips, and more. Everything is saved in one place so you can look back at what you've done.

---

## Social Features

You can also see what your friends are doing on the app. This can help you find new things to try. Connect with friends to share recommendations.

## How It Works

1. **Log** — Add an experience to your profile
2. **Rate** — Give it a rating
3. **Browse** — See what your friends have rated
